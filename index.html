About Us

Hello world.. I’m the entire Blue Whirl team — programmer, artist, sound designer, QA tester, and coffee department all in one.

I make games because therapy is expensive and programming is cheaper. Most of my ideas start as “what if?” and end with “why did I do this to myself?” When I’m not fixing bugs I created, I’m adding new ones on purpose (for features, of course). Every pixel, explosion, and sound is hand-crafted with blood, sweat and tears. This is my sandbox, a one-person studio making games that are sometimes cool, mostly weird, and always built with too much caffeine. Just don’t ask how long it took — I stopped counting months ago.
