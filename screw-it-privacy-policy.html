Privacy Policy for Screw It

Effective Date: January 01, 2026

Welcome to Screw It. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services to connect clients with independent contractors.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

When you create an account as a Client or a Contractor, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication and account management.
  • Name / Business Name: Used to identify you within the platform.
  • Location Data: Clients provide job addresses to receive accurate quotes. Contractors may provide service area locations.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your full credit card or bank account details on our servers.

1.2 Job & Interaction Data

To provide our core marketplace services, we process:

  • Job Details & Photos: Information, descriptions, and media uploaded by clients to outline the required work.
  • Chat Messages: In-app communications between clients and contractors to coordinate jobs securely.
  • Reviews & Ratings: Public feedback provided after a job is completed.

1.3 AI Processing

When clients request a variation to a job's scope of work, the proposed changes are sent to an AI service (Google Gemini API) to generate a clean, revised, and legally structured Scope of Work document. This data is not used to train global AI models.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate connections between clients and verified contractors.
  • Process secure payments and contractor payouts via Stripe Connect.
  • Send push notifications regarding job bids, messages, and project updates.
  • Maintain trust and safety on the platform through review monitoring and administrative oversight.

3. Data Protection & Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL.
  • Authentication: We use Google Firebase Authentication to ensure account security.
  • Financial Security: All financial and payout information is tokenized and stored securely by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For secure database storage, authentication, and cloud functions.
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing, credit card holds, and contractor bank payouts.
  • Expo Application Services: For delivering push notifications to your device.

5. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to control your data:

  • Access Your Data: You can view all your active jobs, bids, and profile information within the app.
  • Delete Your Account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated personal data directly within the app by navigating to the Profile tab and tapping "Delete Account".

6. Contact Us
