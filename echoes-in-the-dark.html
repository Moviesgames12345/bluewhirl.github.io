Echoes In The Dark

Unravel chilling mysteries in a world where shadows whisper and long-forgotten secrets linger.

Discover the Story

Step Into the Unknown

Echoes In The Dark is a narrative-driven psychological horror adventure that will test your courage and wit. Traverse hauntingly atmospheric environments, from derelict manors to fog-laden forests, each meticulously crafted to immerse you in a world teetering on the brink of madness.

Every shadow could hide a threat, every echo could be a clue. Your choices matter, shaping the narrative and leading to one of several unique endings. Can you uncover the truth before the darkness consumes you?

Key Features:

  • Intricate Puzzles: Challenge your mind with complex riddles and environmental puzzles.
  • Branching Narrative: Your decisions directly impact the story and its outcome.
  • Atmospheric Horror: Experience true psychological horror with immersive sound design and visuals.
  • Deep Exploration: Uncover hidden lore, collectibles, and secrets scattered throughout the world.
