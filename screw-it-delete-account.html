Request Account Deletion

We value your privacy. If you wish to permanently delete your Screw It account and all associated personal data, you can do so directly within the mobile app:

  1. Open the Screw It app.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap the Delete Account button at the bottom of the screen.

Can't access the app?

If you no longer have access to the app and wish to request manual account deletion, please email our support team from the email address associated with your account.

Email Support

What data is deleted? Your profile information, personal details, location data, and authentication records are permanently removed. Messages and job histories may be retained in an anonymized format for administrative records, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
